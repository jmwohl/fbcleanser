One in five posts on Facebook is an ad.*

That's too many.

This is a simple little bookmarklet to remove "Suggested Posts" from your feed.

To use it, simply drag this link to your browser's bookmark bar:

Whenever you visit Facebook, click the bookmarklet. Suggested Posts will no longer appear in your feed.
